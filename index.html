Our Story

Why We Exist

After having seen firsthand the devastating impact of ALS, both in the clinic and within our own families, we set out to build something that could meaningfully help patients today. We believe that the future of ALS care lies in personalized medicine rooted in each patient’s own biology.

Using cells directly from the patient, Sui Health creates personalized neuronal models that capture the specific mechanisms driving an individual's disease. These models allow us to test therapies in a way that is deeply personalized and clinically relevant.

Patient-Centered Care

Every decision begins with the patient’s needs. We believe that individuals living with neurodegenerative disease deserve tailored, actionable solutions.

Collaboration

We believe breakthrough discoveries happen when we work together. We are excited to collaborate with academia, industry, and patient communities to accelerate discovery and expand access to personalized interventions.

Ethical Integrity

We ensure patient privacy, informed consent, and responsible use of biological materials. We are committed to doing responsible science.

What We Do

Our Impact

Disease Modeling

We generate neurons directly from each patient’s cells to build personalized models of their disease. These models reveal the specific mechanisms affecting that person’s neurons and allow us to quickly test which treatments might help.

Drug Discovery Platform

Using automated high-throughput assays and longitudinal imaging, we test hundreds of FDA-approved compounds with human safety data to identify treatments tailored to individual disease profiles.

Patient Support

Once we identify personalized therapies, we help bring these treatments back to the patient.

Meet Our Team

The People Behind Our Mission

Dr. Sami Barmada

Co-Founder & Director
Dr. Sami Barmada is a neurologist and professor at the University of Michigan. An MD/PhD by training, he leads research aimed at understanding the molecular and cellular mechanisms of neurodegenerative diseases such as ALS and frontotemporal dementia, with the goal of identifying new therapeutic strategies.

Dr. Tristan McKinney

Co-Founder & Director
Tristan McKinney is a machine learning scientist with a PhD in Theoretical Physics from Caltech. He is passionate about using computational tools to enhance drug screening platforms and accelerate discoveries that advance human health.

Dr. Arpana Arjun McKinney

Co-Founder & Director
Dr. Arpana Arjun McKinney earned her PhD in Developmental and Stem Cell Biology from UCSF. Having witnessed the impact of ALS in her family, she is dedicated to advancing personalized treatments for neurodegenerative diseases.
